NPHC Hudson County Member Quick Start

Top 5 need-to-know items for all members.

1) Access Eligibility

Your email must be on the official Google Group access list maintained by the VP.

2) First-Time Login Verification

On first login, complete the confirmation/security code step to verify your identity and activate access.

3) Save to Home Screen (Use Like an App)

  • iPhone: Open in Safari, tap Share, tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Android: Open in Chrome, tap menu, tap Add to Home screen or Install app.

4) Meeting Materials Are In-App

Meeting agendas, deck materials, governance files, and records are available inside the portal.

5) Share Your Upcoming Events

Use the Event Submission / Social Media forms to request publication and internal calendar placement.
